Why is childhood obesity a social issue?

Chilhood obesity may have impact when the child is not only a kid, but also affect them when they are an adult

Childhood obesity occurs when children are not eating healthy food and having beverages like coke, pepsi, etc. (not water) which is bad because the fatty food not burnt is accumilated in the body which if accumilated a lot, then will affect the child's performance in sports and other physical activities, hence being not the best practise for cardiovascular fitness(for the heart) which mat cause heart diseases like cardiovascular arrest(heart attack). In addition to that, it is a culprit for hypertension cancer, gall bladder diseases, etc.

BUT there is a happy news!! These diseases can be avoided by eating healthy food and excercising or be phisically active regularly like playing(on the playground) for about an hour.
