Obesity, a social issue


What is obesity ?

Obesity is phenomena in which the body stores more fats than it burns and it accumilates more than it should in the body. Obesity starts from

  • Healthy Weight
  • Overweight
  • Obese

Being overweight is not always that bad because it could be extra weight due to bones or/and muscles. Overweight or obese is bad when the extra weight is created by fats.
